Pizza Margherita

Description

Pizza Margherita is one of the most popular Italian pizzas. It combines tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil for a simple but delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 150 ml tomato sauce
  • 200 g mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Bake for 12–15 minutes.
  7. Add fresh basil before serving.
  8. Slice and enjoy.
