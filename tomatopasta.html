Tomato Pasta

Description

Tomato Pasta is a simple and delicious Italian-inspired dish made with pasta, tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, and herbs. It is quick to prepare and perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 250 g pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400 g canned tomatoes
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Fresh parsley (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add the garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Add the tomatoes, basil, salt, and black pepper.
  6. Simmer the sauce for 10–15 minutes.
  7. Drain the pasta and add it to the sauce.
  8. Toss everything together until the pasta is well coated.
  9. Serve with fresh parsley and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
