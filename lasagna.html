Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It is a comforting meal perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500 g ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 500 ml tomato sauce
  • 250 g mozzarella cheese
  • 100 g Parmesan cheese
  • 500 ml béchamel sauce
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles.
  3. Cook the beef with onion and garlic.
  4. Add the tomato sauce.
  5. Layer noodles, meat sauce, béchamel, and cheese.
  6. Repeat the layers.
  7. Bake for 40 minutes.
  8. Let it rest before serving.
